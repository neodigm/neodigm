Horizontal Scroll Effect

An immersive UX experience, this scroll effect differentiates from linear top-down sites. The content appears from left to right as controlled by the mouse wheel or scroll bar.

Snap to Content

When the end user stops scrolling the content is snapped into place so that a single card can be focused. Meaning that the user will not be looking at a half or one card and a half of another.
