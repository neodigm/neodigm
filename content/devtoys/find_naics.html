The NAICS Number Finder

The NAICS numbering system is a five / six-digit code at the most detailed level. The first 5 digits are the same in all three countries. The first two digits designate the largest business sector; the third digit represents the subsector; the fourth digit represents the industry group; the fifth digit designates the NAICS industries, and the sixth digit represents the national industries.
