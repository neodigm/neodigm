MacBook Setup for the Pros 2023

Setting up a new MacBook for software development can be tricky. Here I provide the simple basics to get you started.

I realize that some developers may be migrating from Windows to OSX for the first time and that can be difficult. Especially when onboarding as a new employee. Every development environment is unique but the main apps that you want working fast are Finder, Git, Node, NPM, Java, SFDX, Shell, VSCode, and xCode.

Whenever possible I will provide a verification step to make sure the command worked. I've also included a list of the most productive Chrome extensions, Progressive Web Apps, and VSCode extensions. I will try to keep these instructions up to date with the most recent changes.

A quick note on cleaning your new Macbook. Please follow Apple's recommended method for cleaning your screen.

Frequently Asked New Macbook Questions


Does My Macbook Use an Intel or Apple Chip?

Simply click on the Apple on top of your screen and select "About this Mac". I found this helpful when trying to install Docker desktop. BTW an Apple Chip sounds like a healthy snack.

How do I Clean my Macbook?

Begin by shutting down your Macbook and unplugging it. Better safe than sorry. Use a damp lint-free cloth to clean the exterior. Do not get moisture inside any openings. DON'T spray liquid directly on the computer. Don't use aerosol sprays, solvents, abrasives, or cleaners containing hydrogen peroxide that might damage the finish.
