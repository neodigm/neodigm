Neodigm 55 is an eclectic JavaScript UX micro-library. Our lightweight components come together in a unique way that will make visiting your website playful and fun.

Neodigm 55 is low-code, performant, accessible, and open source. Because it’s so easy to get started this is the perfect solution for quick landing pages or event sites.

Simple Popup




Simple Toast



Neodigm Toast is a super convenient way to show a quick message without disrupting the user’s flow. It just pops-up at the bottom of the screen and shows a message, then animates off the screen.

Its unobtrusive nature makes it good for dynamic messages that need to be seen but do not need to occupy precious screen real-estate.

Neodigm Popup

Hi, I am a simple popup. I stay fixed when the page scrolls. I stay focused because the background is blurred. I size myself automatically to fit your content. I am easy to open and easy to close.

I can be added to your website without knowledge of JavaScript.
