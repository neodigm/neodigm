MacBook Setup for the Pros

Setting up a new MacBook for software development can be tricky. Here I provide the simple basics to get you started.

I realize that some developers may be migrating from Windows to OSX for the first time and that can be difficult. Especially when onboarding a new employee. Every development environment is unique but the main apps that you want working fast are Finder, Node, NPM, Git, Java, Scala, Shell, VSCode and xCode.
