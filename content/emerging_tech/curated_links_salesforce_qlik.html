Where is your data?

Not all your business data belongs in Salesforce. Replicating unnecessary data without a specific business reason will just bloat the size of your Salesforce instance and increase storage costs.

Saleforce Connectors

The Qlik Salesforce Connector enables QlikView and Qlik Sense to efficiently load data into an app from a Salesforce.com data set. You must have a Salesforce.com account with current access credentials to access data. Once a connection to a Salesforce.com account has been created, data can be selected from tables available to the user's account and then loaded into an app where it can be used in visualizations.

You can also onboard JIRA data to Qlik Cloud Data Services.
