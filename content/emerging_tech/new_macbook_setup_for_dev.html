Setting up a new MacBook for frontend development can be tricky. I realize that some developers may be migrating from Windows to OSX for the first time and that can be daunting. Especially when onboarding a new employee.

Every development environment is just a little bit unique but the main apps that you want working are Finder, Node, NPM, Git, Java, Scala, Shell, VSCode and xCode. Here I provide the basics to get you started.
